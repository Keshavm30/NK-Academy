Best Science Classes In Mumbai

Science is a fundamental discipline for First Year Junior College (FYJC) and Second Year Junior College (SYJC) students, serving as a gateway to understanding the natural world. It is a multifaceted field encompassing subjects such as physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics, offering students a comprehensive view of the universe's workings.

FYJC and SYJC students studying science delve into the intricacies of the physical world, exploring the laws that govern matter, energy, and the forces that shape our environment. They uncover the chemical reactions that underpin life and matter, gaining insights into the composition of substances and their properties.

Biology, another vital component of science, unravels the mysteries of life itself, from the smallest microorganisms to complex ecosystems. Students learn about the intricacies of living organisms, genetics, and the interplay between different species.

Mathematics, an integral part of science, equips students with essential problem-solving skills, enabling them to analyse data, make predictions, and understand complex scientific concepts.

In essence, science empowers FYJC and SYJC students to think critically, ask questions, and explore the world through an evidence-based approach. It lays the foundation for future scientific pursuits, preparing them for diverse career paths, from medicine and engineering to research and environmental science.

We at NK Academy believe that science fosters intellectual curiosity and a deep appreciation for the wonders of the natural world.

HSC

Maharashtra Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination is Std. XII (HSC) board exam conducted by MSBSHSE. This exam is conducted twice in a year Feb or March & 2nd Attempt immediately in the month of June or July.

HSC Board Exam is the gateway for various new-age B.Sc. courses which are in high demand worldwide. To get admission for B.Sc. top colleges HSC Board Exam score play very important role. Apart from this the score of HSC Board Exam also helps the students to get admission in Architecture, Merchant Navy, NDA, Para-Medical Courses, various diploma courses after Std. XII and etc.

- Batches commence from April 2023 to April 2024
- 3 to 4 lectures a day
- Duration of every lecture: 1 to 2 hours
- Semester wise portion completion.
- Grade wise tests and remedials.

Engineering

Engineering is one of the discipline of science which uses scientific applications for betterment of human beings. The future could not have been any better. Engineers today are shaping the landscape of contemporary life with innovations and ideas. As an engineer, you can create a robot that manages the road traffic management, helps provide security solutions for the national defence, increase the food productivity with natural farming or launch satellites to space. As an environmental engineer, you can solve the urban sewage problems, design sustainable transport mechanism, or create newer sources of renewable energy. As an IT engineer, you can design applications that maintain longevity of life, connect citizens with government initiatives, and so forth.

JEE assesses candidates' knowledge in subjects such as physics, chemistry, and mathematics. Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test (MHT-CET) is a highly regarded state-level entrance examination conducted in Maharashtra, India. It serves as a gateway for students seeking admission to various undergraduate programs, including engineering, pharmacy, and health sciences, in Maharashtra colleges and universities. MH-CET is known for its rigorous evaluation and standardised testing procedures, assessing students' knowledge and aptitude in subjects like physics, chemistry, mathematics, and biology, depending on their chosen field of study. Medical

Medical is the discipline of science which practices establishing the diagnosis, prognosis, treatment, and prevention of disease. Medicine encompasses a variety of health care practices evolved to maintain and restore health by the prevention and treatment of illness. The field of Medicine is undergoing tremendous development & embracing technology, innovations and ideas to improve the overall healthcare facilities.The doctors of future have to address the newer challenges, find solutions for incurable diseases, invent drugs that have limited to no side effects, etc.

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate) (or NEET UG), is a highly competitive medical entrance examination in India. Conducted annually by the National Testing Agency (NTA), NEET is a standardised test for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate medical and dental courses (MBBS and BDS) in India and abroad. NEET assesses students' knowledge in physics, chemistry, and biology, including botany and zoology. It serves as the primary criterion for admission to medical colleges across the country, replacing various state-level entrance exams. NEET has become a crucial examination, drawing thousands of aspiring medical professionals seeking to secure a seat in prestigious medical institutions and fulfil their dreams of becoming doctors.

- Batches commence from April 2023 to April 2024.
- 3 to 4 lectures a day.
- Duration of every lecture:2 hours.
- Intense practice tests and preparation with experts.
- Remedial sessions and career guidance.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy is the clinical health science that links medical science with chemistry and it is charged with the discovery, production, disposal, safe and effective use, and control of medications and drugs. The practice of pharmacy requires excellent knowledge of drugs, their mechanism of action, side effects, interactions, mobility and toxicity.
