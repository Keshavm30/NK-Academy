About Us

Nand Kishor Mishra
Our Founder

'Learning from the best teachers is an experience that can sometimes be more profound than education itself.'

This is precisely what we, at NK Academy have on our palette. Brilliant teachers, perfect notes and loads of practice are the key elements we nurture at NK Academy. We have always believed in hard work as goes the saying 'There is no elevator to success, we have to take the stairs.'
'The best is yet to come' is what we cherish in our mind and feed our students. This leads to continuous and competitive improvement.

It is our perennial effort to make the best of technology available at fingertips.

We are committed to deliver highest quality education!
